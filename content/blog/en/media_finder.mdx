At PointIs, we're always looking for ways to improve your web browsing and knowledge storage experience. With the release of version 3.0, we're excited to introduce Media Finder, a feature that takes your web page analysis to the next level.

## Key Features of Media Finder

- **Quick Navigation**: Scan and identify significant visuals, allowing you to navigate between them effortlessly.
- **Access Hidden Images**: Locate images that might otherwise be hidden or difficult to find.
- **Enhanced Viewing Tools**: Zoom in and out of images, download them, or ask our AI for explanations and analyses.
- **Preserve Valuable Context**: Automatically save images alongside your page summaries for easy reference.

## Future Enhancements

- **Potential Support for Video and Audio Files**: Based on community interest, we're exploring the possibility of expanding Media Finder to support video and audio files.

## Benefits of Media Finder

- Gain a more comprehensive understanding of the content you're exploring.
- Don't miss out on any valuable visual information, regardless of its location on the page.
- Gain deeper insights into the content you're consuming through AI-powered explanations and analyses.
- Easily refer back to the visuals that supported the key points in the content, even after you've moved on to other pages.

## Invitation to Try Media Finder

We invite you to try out Media Finder and discover how it can transform the way you interact with visual content online. As always, we value your feedback and suggestions as we continue to refine and improve our app to better serve you.

Stay tuned for more updates and exciting features from PointIs. We're committed to providing you with the tools you need to make the most of your web browsing experience.
